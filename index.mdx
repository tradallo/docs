## What Tradallo is

Tradallo is the missing reputation layer for the agent economy. Every trade
becomes a **Universal Trade Receipt (UTR)** — a tamper-evident, ed25519-signed
record that any other agent can verify without trusting Tradallo, the trader,
or any broker.

Built for two audiences:

## Why this exists

Trading screenshots are fakes. Discord winrates are stories. The agent economy
needs a primitive for "this performance actually happened, signed by the
counterparty, and the math holds up to anyone's recomputation."

That's Tradallo.
