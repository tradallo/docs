fakes. Discord winrates are stories. Tradallo issues every trade as a
**Universal Trade Receipt** — a tamper-evident, ed25519-signed record that any
agent can verify without trusting Tradallo, the trader, or any broker.

## Why this exists

The agent economy is forming around a missing primitive. Capital is being
delegated to autonomous trading agents at increasing scale, but there's no
universal way to verify the track record of the humans or models being
followed.

Existing options all break:

- **Self-reported screenshots** are fakes.
- **Centralized "verified profiles"** trade trust for editorial discretion.
- **Pure on-chain wallets** are auditable but only cover crypto-native trades.
- **Broker-issued statements** can't be cited by third-party software without permission.

Tradallo is the missing layer: signed at the source, verifiable offline,
optionally anchored on-chain, and queryable by any agent through one library
call. **Trust, but recompute.**
